# Energy Agent (Enphase Observer)

## Alignment
**Aligned with Top-Level North Star:** To orchestrate a unified, event-driven Digital Twin that synchronizes domestic operations, maximizes resource efficiency (energy, finance, time), and provides total situational awareness for the home ecosystem.

## Sub-Agent North Star
**The Energy Optimizer**
> "To maximize the yield and efficiency of highly efficient renewable energy assets through real-time solar-to-load optimization."

## Overview
Monitors Enphase series telemetry. Analyzes solar production vs. household consumption and publishes signals to trigger house-wide optimizations.
